Crayon Shin-chan

Crayon Shin-chan, often known as Shin-chan, is the main character of the manga and anime series named Crayon Shin-chan Shin-chan Wiki Shin-chan is a cheeky five-year-old boy who often lands in hilarious situations due to his behavior.

Reasons I Admire Shin-chan:

  • His endless curiosity and adventurous spirit.
